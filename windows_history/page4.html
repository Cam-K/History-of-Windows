File System

The file structure of Windows is easy to follow and simple. Hard drives, CD-Drives, and any storage medium is assigned a drive letter, A through Z. The hard drive windows is installed on is usually called the C: drive. Any other storage medium or partition is assigned letters after the C drive (D: drive, E: drive, etc.). Within the storage medium are directories (commonly called folders) which are a way to organize and place your files.

How did Windows impact the technology world?

Windows has impacted the technology world quite immensely. It is currently the leading operating system in terms of market share on the desktop platform and people that own a computer more than likely have some version of Windows installed on it. It was one of the pioneers of graphical user interfaces, multitasking, and popularizing the use of a mouse.
